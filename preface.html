PrefaceLast updated: 26-07-2021

I've always wanted to make my own video game, a world I could call my own, characters that relate to my own life.

Growing up with an engineering father, my gameboy and an vivid imagination, were all contributers in creating the man I am today. It's fuelled my passion of game development, my constant fascination with indie developers and reared me into a world of game dev.

My background was never in game development, whilst I grew up playing games, I never thought of the prospect of game development until I was in my third of university.

I studied Geology, a far cry from anything software related. For some reason it never clicked. I never felt any passion from my degree but I didn't know what I wanted to do with my life.

That's when I started to code. What felt like a chore at the start, turned into something I actively spent all my free time on. I enjoyed solving problems, developing solutions that were both efficient and thorough. I've worked on drones, to home automation, through to my own personal projects all in C#. I pushed through the Mt Stupid, of the Dunning-Krugger effect and whilst I don't consider myself the best software programmer of all time, I know enough to implement solutions on a myriad of tasks.

This document is an ode to Peblo and myself, showing the journey from conception to creation. A cumilation of my present and past, which I hope you see portraid in my art.

I hope you enjoy this document.

- Will Dye
