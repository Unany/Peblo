Game Concept

Introduction

If you could fight fate? Would you?

Peblo is a Top-Down action-adventure game for the PC with the intent to launch on the nintendo switch. Peblo was a clay doll created by Eva, brought to life to fufill his fate by a 'god'.

Background

Peblo will aim to be a franchise, exploring the relationship between Eva and Peblo and what fate has in store for them. The game will be built using Unity and its scriptable rendering pipeline URP.

As it's a new game, with a lot of technical methods to implement, in order to make the game achievable the top-done engine asset by More Mountains is being used as the core.

Having been developed for years, with good documentation - it is the perfect based to start developing a top-down game. I will then add additional fucntionality on top of information.

A lot of the core concepts will be brough across from the Legend of Zelda series but changed in order to make it more original and unique than that franchise.

Description

Peblo, places the player in an unknown world, with no direction and questions without answers.

It is the players job to unravel what their fate is, why they were brought to life and what the voices are, be that helping others are letting others sucumb to their fate.

The few things the player can avoid is their own impending fate and the truth of Peblo's creation.

Key Features

Peblo will allow the player to explore the fragmented world, and piece together what happened. From an empty shell, to a full vessel, I hope that the player can experience who peblo is and find comfort in the fact that we all carry a seed of change.

I really want the Peblo world to feel rich and alive, its a perfect contrast to something which is so empty to start with. In order to achieve this, I want to really use physics to make every second in the game feel impactful. From Dynamic Water, to vivid vegetation and players interaction with the environment. It should feel special.

A dynamic story will allow for playability. Each character has several fates, some fixed, some changeable. This leads to different endings.

Peblo will have two difficulties. This is to ensure, extra challenges to veteran gamers, whilst newer gamers can pick up the game with ease.


Genre

Peblo is a Third-person action-adventure game. Closely, following the genre of the Legend of Zelda series.

Platform

The targeted platform is the Nintendo Switch, with the game being built for PC and then ported across. I want the nintendo switch to be the primary platform.
